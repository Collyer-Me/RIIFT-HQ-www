The core of the board

RIIFT tracks integrate seamlessly with your existing shaper and any foil kit. No drag, no compromise. Ride it unpowered, as a perfectly balanced foil-assist, or transform your existing setup into the world’s best e-foil.  One board. Every discipline. RIIFT does it all.

One system. Infinite possibilities.

Have RIIFT Tracks engineered into your next board and you've instantly got a full carbon deck-to-baseplate connection like nothing you've ever felt. Tracks come in 2P and 4P sizes; the PowerPack comes in 2P, 3P and 4P. All three packs fit flush in 4P tracks — only the 2P pack fits 2P tracks.

Then power up when you're ready: the RIIFT Power System delivers an intuitive assist for uncatchable waves and downwind voyages, or a full e-foil for any flat day. No waves? No wind? No excuses.

Three ways to ride. One board.
