01 — Brand at a glance

Engineered calm, ocean energy

RIIFT is an efoil propulsion system in two parts — carbon Tracks engineered into a board by a shaper, and a clip-in Power System. The brand voice is confident and technical but never cold: precision engineering in service of pure ride feel.

The visual language is deliberately monochrome and restrained — black, white and a warm off-white — so that full-bleed ocean photography carries all the colour and emotion. Type is structural and uppercase; layouts are generous and quiet. The product is the hero; the system gets out of the way.

Monochrome

Black, white, off-white. Photography supplies the colour.

Structural

Uppercase Poppins headlines, tight tracking, clear hierarchy.

Cinematic

Full-bleed imagery, dark scrims, slow reveals.

Primary tagline

Drag Free. Tool-less. Pure Performance.

Always phrased positively — never "no drag, no tools".

02 — Logo

The wordmark

An italic, heavyweight wordmark. It is the sole brand mark in use — there is no separate symbol/icon yet. Two colourways: white for dark or photographic backgrounds, black for light.

Placement & sizing

  • Header height 24px; footer 24px; clear space ≥ the cap height on all sides.
  • Left-aligned in the nav, top-left in the footer.
  • Never below ~18px tall — legibility of the italic falls off.

Don't

  • Recolour, add effects, or set it upright (it is intentionally italic).
  • Place the white mark on a busy light area without a scrim.
  • Rebuild it in live text — always use the supplied PNG.

⚑ Decision needed

Current logo is a raster PNG extracted from the style brief. We need vector (SVG/EPS) masters, a defined minimum size, and a view on whether a standalone symbol/app-icon is required.

05 — Components

The building blocks

Buttons

Pill · light Solid · dark Text link →

Pill (radius 999px) is the primary CTA, usually white-on-photo. Solid square is the form/subscribe action. Text links nudge their arrow on hover (gap 8→14px). Hover lifts buttons −2px / darkens #111→#333.

Cards

Eyebrow

Card title

White surface, optional hairline border, image on top. Lifts −4px with a soft shadow on hover.

Other patterns

  • Stat / advantage column — 2px top rule in Ink, title + body.
  • Spec row — label left (#999), value right, hairline divider.
  • Form input — 1px #ccc border, square, no radius.
  • Play button — white circle, ink triangle.

Corner radius is minimal — cards are near-square (≈2px); only pills and circular controls are fully rounded.

Navigation & footer

07 — Motion

Calm, deliberate movement

  • Scroll reveal — fade in + rise 28px, 700ms, ease cubic-bezier(.2,.7,.3,1), triggered at 12% in view.
  • Kinetic headline — hero h1 cycles three phrases every 2.6s with a 400ms fade/slide.
  • Hover — cards lift −4px + soft shadow; buttons −2px; image tiles zoom to 1.06 over 600ms.
  • Principle — one thing moves at a time; nothing bounces. Motion supports reading, never decorates.

08 — Voice & tone

Confident, positive, precise

Do

  • Phrase positively — "Drag Free", not "No Drag".
  • Short, declarative, uppercase headlines.
  • Lead with ride feel; back it with engineering.
  • Use the real numbers (60s / 30s, Wh, prices).

Don't

  • Stack negatives or hype ("game-changing", "revolutionary").
  • Over-explain — let the photography breathe.
  • Mix title case into headings.
  • Alter product names or specs (see next section).

09 — Product nomenclature & facts (fixed)

Don't reword these

Names, numbers and the compatibility rule are set by engineering and legal. Style them, but keep the wording and values exact.

The two parts

RIIFT Tracks — carbon tracks laminated into a board by a shaper (2P & 4P). RIIFT Power System — PowerPack + Propulsion Unit + case/chargers/remote (2P / 3P / 4P).

Compatibility rule

All three PowerPacks fit flush in 4P tracks. 2P tracks accept only the 2P pack. Modes swap in 60s, batteries in 30s, all tool-less.
